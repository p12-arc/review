Top Issues

The main complaint themes detected in the sample data.

Delivery Delay

Appears in multiple negative reviews. This is the strongest operational issue in the dataset.

Packaging Damage

Visible in both direct complaints and combined negative feedback. Impacts trust quickly.

Sizing / Fit

Frequent mixed-sentiment issue. Often causes returns or uncertainty before purchase.

Wrong Item

High urgency complaint that typically requires immediate support escalation.

Color Mismatch

Expectation gap between product images and received item. Important for conversion quality.

Instructions / Usability

Not always negative, but can cause friction and reduce first-time satisfaction.

AI Summary

What this sample dataset suggests

Product quality is generally well received, especially for Product B and the positive delivery experience on Product C. However, Product A shows the strongest risk signal because of delivery delay, damaged packaging, and wrong item complaints. Product C has mixed feedback, mainly related to sizing and usability. If this were live data, the best first action would be to alert operations about fulfillment issues and improve size guidance for customers.
